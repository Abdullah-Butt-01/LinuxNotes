## Commands Practiced

* curl - used to check service is working or not

## How I fixed

## Key Learnings

* To exit from the "ping", press Control+C.
* ping:
	- Checks network reachability
	- Does not check apps
* curl:
	-  Checks application/service responce
	- used for APIs and web servers
